DataGenerator Hackathon!

 11/15/2014 @FINRA MD Campus

Overview

DataGenerator 2.0 is here! This version represents a 9-month, complete rewrite based on open standards and Big Data scalability. Join the DataGenerator team at our Hackathon to learn about the latest features and apply the tool to new and interesting problems. Cash prizes will be given to teams enhancing the tool in the most interesting and impactful ways.

Join us Saturday, November 15th for fun, free food, and a friendly competition!

Rules

1. Self-formed teams can be of any size. (Ideally less than 5 people per team)

2. Each team can work on one or more idea - ONLY the best idea will be scored.

3. An idea can be usability/performance/feature enhancements, new features, application of DataGenerator to solve hard practical problems, ingenious ways to market and monetize it as a product or something completely new. Be creative!

4. Each team will be scored using the following rubric:

Score = Size (1-3) * [Implementation Completeness (0-7) + Creativity (0-20) + Documentation/Clarity/Presentation (0-3)]

5. Grand prize winner will be the team with the highest score

6. Have fun!

7. Network!
